Primary Explanatory Independent Variables

  1. Hhveh- How many working vehicles (cars, trucks, and motorcycles) are available in your household? This ranges from 0 to 3.
  2. Walkrate- Rate the quality of walkability/accessibility in your community (scale 1-5, very poor to very good).
  3. Transitqual- I created this variable based on transitavail (whether there is public transit) and transitrate (quality of public transit).The value=0, if there is no public transit and then for those who do have public transit in their community, the values range from 1-5.
# Creating the perceived public transit quality variable based on transitavail and transitrate
publictransit<-publictransit%>%
  mutate(pt_qual=case_when(
    ptavail==0~0,
    ptavail==1 & transitrate==1~1,
    ptavail==1 & transitrate==2~2,
    ptavail==1 & transitrate==3~3,
    ptavail==1 & transitrate==4~4,
    ptavail==1 & transitrate==5~5
  ))

# Linear regression with interaction effects
lm1<-lm(lifesat~hhveh*ptqual+type*ptqual+walk_fac+ hhincscale+age+educ+poc+male, data=publictransit)
